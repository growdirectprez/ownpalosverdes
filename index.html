Meet Angelique

I know every street,
every school zone,
every sunset view.

I moved to the Palos Verdes Peninsula in 2003, before the coffee shops, before the trail maps, before anyone was writing neighborhood guides. I learned this place the old-fashioned way — walking the streets, attending the school board meetings, sitting in traffic on Hawthorne Boulevard, and raising my family here.

That's what you get when you work with me. Not a sales pitch — a real conversation with someone who lives here. I'll tell you which streets flood in January, which schools actually deliver on their test scores, and where to find the best tide pools when the kids need to burn off energy.
