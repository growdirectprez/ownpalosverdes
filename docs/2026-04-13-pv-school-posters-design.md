# PVPUSD School Posters — Design Spec

> **Date:** 2026-04-13
> **Purpose:** AI image generation prompts for vintage travel poster-style art, one per PVPUSD campus + key private schools
> **Usage:** OwnPalosVerdes.com school guide page, neighborhood pages (school anchors), print collateral
> **Style:** Same National Park travel poster aesthetic as neighborhood posters — rich, saturated, illustrative, frameable

---

## Shared Style Direction

Same visual language as the neighborhood poster series:
- **Style:** Vintage National Park travel poster — rich illustrative landscapes, saturated color, painterly depth
- **Composition:** Vertical 2:3, school campus as the landscape — buildings, grounds, and surrounding environment treated with the same grandeur as a national park vista
- **Typography:** Bold school name at top, mascot name or "PVPUSD · PALOS VERDES PENINSULA" at bottom
- **Color mood:** Each school gets a dominant palette inspired by its school colors + surrounding landscape
- **Lighting:** Golden hour warmth — these schools feel like destinations, not institutions
- **Details:** School architecture, athletic fields, signature trees, ocean views where applicable. The campus rendered as a place you'd want to be.
- **Border:** Same cream border with rounded corners as neighborhood series

## Production Notes

### Consistency with Neighborhood Series

- Same vertical 2:3 format
- Same border treatment (cream border, rounded corners)
- Same typography system (bold name at top, subtitle at bottom)
- Same saturated, illustrative, National Park poster style
- Same golden hour lighting approach

### School-Specific Additions

- **Rating badge:** Where applicable, a small circular badge in the corner with GreatSchools rating (like a National Park seal)
- **Mascot integration:** School mascot name included in the subtitle bar, mascot animal can be subtly integrated (a sea lion on a rock, a hawk in the sky, a horse on a trail) but should NOT dominate — the campus and landscape are the heroes
- **Feeder pattern visual:** Consider a "collector's series" treatment — elementary posters are slightly smaller format, intermediates medium, high schools largest — visual hierarchy mirrors the school progression

### Usage

- **School guide page:** Posters as hero images for each school section
- **Neighborhood pages:** The local elementary school poster appears on its neighborhood page, reinforcing the school-neighborhood connection
- **Lead magnet:** "The PVPUSD School Poster Collection" — gated download alongside the neighborhood posters
- **Open house collateral:** Print the relevant school poster for open houses in that school's attendance boundary — parents with kids are the highest-intent buyers
- **Feeder pattern infographic:** All 15 posters arranged in a pyramid/flow showing elementary → intermediate → high school feeders
